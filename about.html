about me

I graduated from the University of Kentucky in 2014 with a degree in Mechanical Engineering. After realizing my passion for design, I decided to go back to school and attend the University of Cincinnati to pursue a degree in Industrial Design (2020). I'd like to use my interest in technology to bring new, exciting, and thoughtful products into the world.
